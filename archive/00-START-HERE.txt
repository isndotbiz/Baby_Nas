TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

"Network share not accessible"
  → Check: ping 172.21.203.18
  → Check: VPN connection active
  → Check: Firewall allows port 445

"Permission denied"
  → Verify: SMB credentials are correct
  → Check: TrueNAS share permissions
  → Try: Re-run script with /NoGUI flag

"Veeam service not running"
  → Run: Get-Service VeeamEndpointBackupSvc
